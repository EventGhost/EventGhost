Rate
speaking speed in words per minute. Values 80 to 450.
Volume
volume in range 0-200 or more. 0=silence, 100=normal full volume, greater values may produce amplitude compression or distortion
Pitch
base pitch, range 0-100. 50=normal
Range
pitch range, range 0-100. 0-monotone, 50=normal
Punctuation
which punctuation characters to announce. 0=none, 1=all, 2=some
Capitals
announce capital letters by: 0=none, 1=sound icon, 2=spelling, 3 or higher, by raising pitch. This values gives the amount in Hz by which the pitch of a word raised to indicate it has a capital letter.
Wordgap
pause between words, units of 10ms (at the default speed)
